Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you register on ThinkFlow, we collect your username, email address, and password (stored securely using bcrypt hashing). We do not collect payment information, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

We also automatically collect limited usage data such as page views and the posts you interact with (likes, comments) to improve the platform experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display your username on posts and comments you author
  • To send important account-related communications (not marketing)
  • To improve the platform and debug issues

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a MongoDB database. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and are never stored in plain text. We use JWT tokens for authentication, which expire after 7 days.

4. Third-Party Services

ThinkFlow does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We do not use advertising networks or tracking pixels.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time. To request account deletion, contact us at our contact page.

Cookie Policy

KeyPurposeExpires
blog_tokenJWT authentication token to keep you logged in7 days

2. What We Do NOT Use

  • ❌ No third-party tracking cookies
  • ❌ No advertising or analytics cookies
  • ❌ No session cookies shared with external services
  • ❌ No fingerprinting or cross-site tracking

You can clear your stored data at any time by signing out of ThinkFlow, or by clearing your browser's localStorage through your browser's developer tools or settings.
